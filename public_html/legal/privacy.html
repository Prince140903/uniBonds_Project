Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 8 October 2025

General

Unibonds Securities ("Unibonds", "we", "us", or "our") is a broking entity that facilitates investments in debt instruments such as corporate bonds, government securities, and other investment products. As a financial services intermediary, We collect, process, and store personal and financial data of individuals in order to comply with regulatory obligations and deliver efficient, secure services.

This Privacy Policy sets out how Unibonds Securities collects, uses, discloses, and protects Your personal data when You interact with Our services. These services are made available through Our digital infrastructure, including but not limited to Our website (www.unibondss.in), mobile applications, APIs, communication interfaces, and associated technologies (collectively, the "Platform"). The Platform enables users to access and use investment-related services offered by Unibonds Securities—such as account onboarding, KYC verification, execution of transactions in debt instruments, investment management tools, and customer support. This Privacy Policy applies to all users of the Platform, including retail investors, high-net-worth individuals, prospective customers, service partners, and any individual whose data is submitted or collected in connection with Our services.

We are committed to safeguarding the privacy of Our users and ensuring compliance with applicable laws and regulations, including the Information Technology Act, 2000, the Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules, 2011, SEBI regulations, and other relevant legal frameworks applicable in India.

As a registered financial intermediary, We are required to collect sensitive personal and financial data including KYC documents, identity proof, investment details, and transaction history. This information is necessary for account creation, regulatory compliance, order execution, transaction processing, and customer support.

By accessing or using the Platform, or by submitting Your data voluntarily, You agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy and authorize Unibonds to collect, use, share, and retain Your personal information in the manner described herein. If You do not agree with this policy, You are advised to discontinue use of Our services.

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time based on legal or operational requirements. We encourage users to review this document periodically to stay informed about how their data is handled. The latest version will always be available on Our website.

This policy should be read in conjunction with Our Terms of Use, User Agreement, and any product-specific disclosures You may receive during onboarding.

By accessing Our Platform, registering an account, submitting Your personal or financial information, or using any of Our services—whether through Our website, mobile application, or offline processes—You provide Your free, informed, specific, and unambiguous consent for the collection, processing, use, storage, and sharing of Your personal data as described in this Privacy Policy.

In compliance with applicable data protection laws, You have the right to provide or withdraw consent for the processing of Your personal data. By submitting Your data to us, such as PAN, Aadhaar, bank account details, KYC documents, or investment preferences, You are consenting to its use for lawful purposes including account onboarding, regulatory compliance, transaction execution, reporting, and customer service.

Your consent is obtained through clear, affirmative actions at the point of data entry—whether via digital forms, onboarding workflows, or paper-based submissions—and is revocable at any time. Where applicable, We may also request Your explicit and separate consent for non-essential activities such as marketing communications or investor outreach programs. You may withdraw Your consent at any time by writing to support@unibonds.in. However, please be aware that withdrawing consent essential for regulatory or contractual purposes may impact Your ability to use Our services.

By continuing to use the Platform, You acknowledge that You have read, understood, and voluntarily agreed to the terms of this Privacy Policy and authorize Unibonds Securities to process Your data in accordance with the applicable laws.

How We Collect Data from You

The methods through which We collect Your data include:

We also receive personal and regulatory data from authorized and compliant external sources, such as:

Use of Cookies

We, Our third-party service providers and Our partners may send "cookies" to Your computer or use similar technologies to enhance Your online experience at Our Platform. "Cookies" are files that can identify You as a unique customer and store Your personal preferences as product preferences to tells us which site pages You have visited and in what order. We use cookies and other technical information to personalise Your visit to the Platform, to analyse traffic on the Platform and to track user trends, patterns and selections for authorised download and for technical reasons connected with Your use of Our Platform.

Cookies can either be permanent (i.e. they remain on Your computer until You delete them) or temporary (i.e. they last only until You close Your browser). We may store both temporary or permanent 'cookies' on Your computer/device to store certain data (that is not sensitive personal information). You may choose to opt out of non-essential cookies through Your browser settings. Essential cookies necessary for platform functionality may still be used. You can configure Your computer's browser to alert You when We attempt to send You a cookie with an option to accept or refuse the cookie.
